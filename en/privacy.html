Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

DealCheck is a supermarket price comparison tool designed with user privacy as a priority — we don't require accounts, we don't have a login system, and we don't send your data to any server.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. No email, no name, no phone number, no IP address logging, no analytics SDK, no telemetry of any kind.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

DealCheck stores the following data inside your browser's local storage only — never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your last 10 price comparisons (product names, prices, and quantities you entered)
  • Your preferred font size

You can delete this data anytime by tapping "Clear all" in the History panel, or by clearing your browser's site data.

3. Google AdSense Advertising

DealCheck displays ads from Google AdSense to keep the service free. Google and third-party vendors:

  • May use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising at www.google.com/settings/ads
  • You can review Google's policies at policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Cookies

DealCheck itself does not set any cookies. However, Google AdSense (see section 3) may set cookies to serve relevant ads.

5. Children's Use

DealCheck does not collect personal data and is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the changes will appear on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We recommend checking back periodically.

7. Contact

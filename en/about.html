About DealCheck

DealCheck is a free, no-download supermarket price comparison tool built in 2026 by Nipitphand, an indie developer based in Thailand. The goal is simple: help every shopper instantly figure out which product is the best deal — without doing mental math in the aisle.

The Problem That Sparked DealCheck

Picture this: you're standing in the supermarket aisle with two dish soaps in front of you. One is a regular 500ml bottle at ฿89. The other is a Buy 1 Get 1 deal — 450ml bottles at ฿159 each. Which is actually cheaper per ml?

Most people either guess, pick the one that "feels" like a deal, or spend 30 seconds doing arithmetic on their phone. None of those options are great.

Supermarkets today run hundreds of simultaneous promotions — BOGO, Buy 2 Get 1, percentage discounts, multi-packs, bundle offers. Products come in dozens of sizes. Comparing real value is genuinely hard without a calculator, and that's exactly what DealCheck solves.

Enter the price and quantity, tap Compare — the app calculates the unit price (per ml, per g, or per piece) for every item and ranks them from cheapest to most expensive. Done in under 5 seconds.

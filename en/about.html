About DealCheck

DealCheck is a supermarket price comparison tool, built in 2026 by Nipitphand, an indie developer in Thailand, to help shoppers make the best buying decisions every time.

The Idea

Shopping in supermarkets often means navigating confusing promotions — Buy 1 Get 1, 40% off, multi-packs, special pricing. Calculating the real deal in your head while walking the aisles isn't easy, especially when products come in different sizes or brands.

DealCheck solves this with a single tool — enter the price and quantity of each item, and the app instantly calculates the unit price and tells you which is cheaper, and by how many percent.

Values

  • ✅ Free forever — no accounts, no subscriptions, no hidden fees
  • 🔒 Privacy-first — no personal data tracking, everything stays on your device (browser local storage)
  • ⚡ Light + fast — no app download, runs directly in your browser
  • 📱 Offline — install as a PWA and use it without internet
  • 🌏 Bilingual — Thai and English support

Why free?

DealCheck shows small banner ads from Google AdSense to support development — but no full-screen pop-ups, no intrusive overlays, and we never sell your personal data.

The goal is to build a real tool people actually use in daily shopping — not to extract money via subscriptions or paywalls.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better: Buy 1 Get 1 or 40% off?

Buy 1 Get 1 equals a 50% discount, so for the same product BOGO always wins. But for different brands or sizes, use DealCheck to compare unit prices.

How do I compare items of different sizes, like 250ml vs 1 liter?

Enter the price and quantity separately, pick the right unit (ml or L), and DealCheck converts and calculates price per ml so you can compare instantly.

What about multi-packs like a 6-can pack — do I need to do math first?

No. Switch on pack mode, enter 6 × 325ml, and the app calculates the total volume for you, then compares it to other items.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes — works in any browser (Safari, Chrome, etc.). No app download required. You can also "Add to Home Screen" to use it like a native app.
